# about

About me
    My name is Kaouane Irania, and I am currently a Master 2 student in Intelligent Application Development and Big Data for the 2024/2025 academic year. Originally from Algeria, I earned a Master's degree in Information Systems Engineering and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Systems before moving to France to further my studies.

    During my Bachelor's degree, I developed membership management software for a sports association, utilizing the Windev development environment for the frontend and Java Spring Boot for the backend. This project helped me gain valuable full-stack development experience.

    In my first year of the Master's program, I completed an internship at Enlilia, where I deepened my knowledge of intelligent application development and big data. This hands-on experience allowed me to work on complex projects while applying the theoretical concepts learned in class.

    Throughout my academic and professional journey, I have acquired skills in various programming languages and technologies, including HTML, CSS, XML, DTT XSLT, PHP, Kotlin, Java, C, Python, and SQL. I have honed these skills through various projects and additional training.
    I am currently seeking new opportunities to apply and expand these skills in a challenging professional environment. The position of SAP Full-Stack Developer at VASPP is an ideal opportunity for me. My expertise in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with my knowledge of PHP, are strengths I am eager to contribute to your team.
